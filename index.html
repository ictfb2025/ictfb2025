About the Conference

The International Conference on Tiling and Fourier Bases (ICTFB 2025) will bring together researchers to discuss the latest developments in tiling theory, Fourier analysis, and related fields.

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: July 15, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: August 30, 2025
  • Conference Dates: October 10-13, 2025

Location

The conference will be held at Xixi University, Chengdu, China.
